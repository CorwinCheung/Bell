# Bell Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 1, 2026

Bell is a simple interval timer. The app is designed to work without an account, sign-in, ads, analytics, or tracking.

## Information We Collect

Bell does not collect personal information.

The app does not require users to create an account, enter a name, provide an email address, or share contact information.

## App Data

Bell may keep recent session history locally while the app is running so the user can see recent practice sessions. This data stays on the user's device and is not sent to a server.

Bell does not currently use cloud sync, backend storage, or cross-device account storage.

## Tracking And Analytics

Bell does not use third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking tools.

Bell does not track users across apps or websites.
